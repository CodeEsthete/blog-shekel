Power of masternodes

Masternode is simply a cryptocurrency full node or computer wallet that keeps the full copy of the blockchain in real-time, just like your have Bitcoin full nodes and is always up & running. These are incentivized nodes that receive rewards based on their availability and their ability to offer network services in a decentralized and trust-less manner. These masternodes are not standalone but they are always communicating with other such nodes to make a decentralized network and are often referred in short form as MN.
